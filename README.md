Our app is your personal AI weather assistant that plans your day for you. Using advanced AI (called LSTM) that spots weather patterns, it doesn’t just show the forecast; it builds your schedule. If you plan to run or do laundry, the app picks the best time based on the weather. If the forecast changes unexpectedly, the app automatically updates your schedule. It also tracks air quality and pollen levels. If the air becomes unhealthy for you, the app sends a friendly alert to your phone so you can stay safe, healthy, and on track with your day."
